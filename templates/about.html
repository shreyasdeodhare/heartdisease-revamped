About HeartGuard AI

Advanced machine learning system for heart disease prediction and risk assessment.

Our Mission

HeartGuard AI is designed to provide accurate, accessible, and actionable heart disease risk assessments using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms. Our goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their heart health.

Advanced Technology

Machine Learning Models

XGBoost, LightGBM, Random Forest, and ensemble methods for superior accuracy.

Class Balancing

SMOTE, ADASYN, and advanced sampling techniques to handle imbalanced datasets.

Feature Selection

Intelligent feature selection using RFE and statistical tests for optimal performance.

Cross-Validation

5-fold stratified cross-validation ensures robust and reliable model performance.
